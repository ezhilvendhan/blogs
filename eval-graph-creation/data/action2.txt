CORPORATE ACTION ANNOUNCEMENTS AND UPDATES
Last Updated: June 15, 2024

MegaRetail Corp (MRET) Announces Stock Split
April 8, 2024 - MegaRetail Corporation announced a 3-for-1 stock split of its common shares, effective May 1, 2024. Post-split, the company's outstanding shares will increase from 5 million to 15 million. The split aims to improve stock liquidity and make shares more accessible to retail investors.

GreenEnergy Solutions (GRNS) Declares Special Cash Dividend
May 2, 2024 - GreenEnergy Solutions' Board has approved a special cash dividend of $2.75 per share, payable on June 1, 2024, to shareholders of record as of May 20, 2024. This represents the company's largest dividend distribution to date, reflecting strong performance in renewable energy projects.

QuantumTech and NanoSystems Announce Strategic Merger
March 15, 2024 - QuantumTech Inc. (QTECH) will acquire NanoSystems Corp (NANO) in an all-stock transaction. NanoSystems shareholders will receive 1.25 QTECH shares for each NANO share. The $3.2 billion merger is expected to close by September 2024, creating a leading quantum computing enterprise.

FoodTech Industries (FDTC) Announces Bonus Share Issue
June 1, 2024 - FoodTech Industries will issue bonus shares in the ratio of 2:1 (two new shares for each existing share). Record date is set for June 20, 2024. The company will issue 8 million new shares, increasing total shares from 4 million to 12 million.

CloudNet Systems (CLND) Declares Special Dividend
May 28, 2024 - CloudNet Systems announced a quarterly dividend of $0.85 per share, representing a 15% increase from the previous quarter. The dividend will be paid on June 30, 2024, to shareholders of record as of June 15, 2024.

GlobalPharma Corp (GPHR) Announces Rights Issue
April 20, 2024 - GlobalPharma Corporation has launched a rights issue offering shareholders the right to purchase one new share for every four shares held at $45 per share. The subscription period runs from May 10 to May 25, 2024. The company aims to raise $750 million to fund research programs.

AutoTech Mobility (AUTM) Implements Stock Split
May 10, 2024 - AutoTech Mobility approved a 4:1 stock split, effective June 1, 2024. The split will increase outstanding shares from 3 million to 12 million. Trading on split-adjusted basis begins June 2, 2024.

SmartFinance Holdings (SFIN) and TechBank Merger
April 5, 2024 - SmartFinance Holdings will merge with TechBank in a $5.8 billion transaction. TechBank shareholders will receive 0.8 SFIN shares for each share held. The merger is expected to complete by Q4 2024.

AgriTech Solutions (AGTS) Announces Bonus Issue
March 25, 2024 - AgriTech Solutions declared a bonus issue of 1:5 (one new share for every five existing shares). The record date is April 15, 2024. The company will issue 3 million new bonus shares.

DataCore Networks (DCNT) Launches Rights Issue
June 10, 2024 - DataCore Networks announced a rights issue in the ratio of 1:3 at a price of $30 per share. The subscription period is from July 1 to July 15, 2024. The company plans to use the proceeds for infrastructure expansion.

MicroFinance Plus (MFIN) Declares Dividend
June 5, 2024 - MicroFinance Plus announced a quarterly dividend of $1.20 per share, payable on July 10, 2024, to shareholders of record as of June 25, 2024. This represents the company's sixth consecutive quarterly dividend increase.

PrecisionMed Corp (PMED) and BioSynth Merger
May 15, 2024 - PrecisionMed Corporation will acquire BioSynth Labs in a stock-for-stock merger valued at $2.1 billion. BioSynth shareholders will receive 1.1 PMED shares for each share held. The transaction is expected to close by end of 2024.
