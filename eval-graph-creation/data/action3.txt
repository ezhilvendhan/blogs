CORPORATE ACTION ANNOUNCEMENTS
Updated: July 1, 2024

SolarTech Solutions (SLRT) Implements Stock Split
June 20, 2024 - SolarTech Solutions' Board has approved a 5:1 stock split. The company's outstanding shares will increase from 2.5 million to 12.5 million shares. Trading on a split-adjusted basis will commence on July 15, 2024. The split reflects SolarTech's strong market performance and aims to enhance stock accessibility.

Quantum Dynamics (QDYN) Declares Record Dividend
June 25, 2024 - Quantum Dynamics announced its largest-ever quarterly dividend of $3.25 per share, payable on August 1, 2024, to shareholders of record as of July 15, 2024. The increased dividend reflects exceptional growth in the quantum computing sector.

MetaVerse Technologies (META) and RealityCore (REAL) to Merge
June 15, 2024 - In a landmark deal, MetaVerse Technologies will acquire RealityCore in a transaction valued at $8.5 billion. RealityCore shareholders will receive 0.95 META shares for each REAL share. The merger is expected to create the world's largest augmented reality company when it closes in December 2024.

CyberShield Corp (CYBR) Announces Rights Issue
June 28, 2024 - CyberShield Corporation launched a rights issue offering shareholders the right to subscribe for one new share for every two shares held at $65 per share. The subscription period is from July 20 to August 5, 2024. The company aims to raise $1.2 billion for cybersecurity infrastructure expansion.

AeroSpace Dynamics (AERO) Declares Stock Split
June 30, 2024 - AeroSpace Dynamics announced a 3:1 stock split, effective August 1, 2024. The split will increase the company's outstanding shares from 6 million to 18 million. The company cited improved trading liquidity as the primary motivation.

HealthTech Innovations (HLTH) Issues Bonus Shares
June 22, 2024 - HealthTech Innovations will issue bonus shares in a 3:2 ratio (three new shares for every two held). The record date is July 10, 2024, with 15 million new shares to be issued, increasing the total from 10 million to 25 million shares.

RoboTech Industries (RBTC) and AI Systems (AISYS) Merger
June 18, 2024 - RoboTech Industries will merge with AI Systems in a $4.2 billion deal. AI Systems shareholders will receive 1.15 RBTC shares for each AISYS share. The merger combines RoboTech's robotics expertise with AI Systems' artificial intelligence capabilities.

OceanClean Energy (OCEN) Announces Special Dividend
June 27, 2024 - OceanClean Energy declared a special dividend of $1.75 per share, payable on July 30, 2024, to shareholders of record as of July 15, 2024. The dividend comes after successful completion of major tidal energy projects.

BioGenetics Plus (BIOG) Launches Rights Issue
June 29, 2024 - BioGenetics Plus announced a rights issue in the ratio of 2:5 at $40 per share. The subscription period runs from July 25 to August 10, 2024. Proceeds will fund expansion of gene therapy research facilities.

SmartGrid Tech (GRID) Implements Stock Split
July 1, 2024 - SmartGrid Tech approved a 6:1 stock split, effective August 15, 2024. The split will increase outstanding shares from 4 million to 24 million. The company aims to make its shares more accessible to retail investors.

MineralCorp Global (MNCG) Announces Bonus Issue
June 24, 2024 - MineralCorp Global declared a bonus issue of 1:1 (one new share for each existing share). The record date is July 20, 2024. The company will double its share count from 7 million to 14 million shares.

FinCloud Services (FNCL) and PayTech Solutions (PTCH) to Merge
June 26, 2024 - FinCloud Services will acquire PayTech Solutions in a stock-for-stock transaction valued at $3.8 billion. PayTech shareholders will receive 0.85 FNCL shares for each PTCH share. The merger is expected to close by November 2024.

NanoMed Research (NANO) Declares Quarterly Dividend
June 28, 2024 - NanoMed Research announced a quarterly dividend of $2.10 per share, representing a 25% increase from the previous quarter. The dividend is payable on August 5, 2024, to shareholders of record as of July 20, 2024.

AgriTech Global (AGGL) Stock Split

DataSphere Solutions (DTSP) Rights Issue
